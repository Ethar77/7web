The Flowers & Candle

Flowers differ considerably in size, shape, colour and arrangement of their parts yet most of them bear a common structural plan. Flowers are symbolic of beauty love and tranquillity. They form the soul of a garden and convey the message of nature to man. We also care about what goes into your space, which is why our candles are hand-poured with 100% natural soy wax and finished with natural cotton wicks. Our candles are cruelty-free, non-toxic, and contain no lead, plastic, paraben, phthalates or synthetic dyes. We handpick our fragrance oils and blend them to create unique scents that bring Middle Eastern aromas to your home, office, or loved ones.
